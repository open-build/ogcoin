Why OGC Doesn't Use Mining

Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, OGC is built on the Stellar network, which uses a different consensus mechanism called the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP).

Energy Efficient

No energy-intensive mining means lower environmental impact

Low Cost

Transaction fees are typically fractions of a penny

Fast Transactions

Transactions confirm in 3-5 seconds on average
