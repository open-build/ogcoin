Safety Rules

Wallet Setup Paths

Different wallets use different labels, but the idea is the same: add a custom Stellar asset, enter OGC, paste the issuer account, then confirm the trustline transaction.

Airdrop Form

LOBSTR

  1. Open Assets or Add Asset.
  2. Search for custom asset if OGC is not listed.
  3. Enter OGC and paste the issuer account.
  4. Confirm the trustline transaction in your wallet.

Freighter

  1. Open Freighter and choose Manage Assets.
  2. Add a custom asset on public network.
  3. Enter OGC and the issuer account.
  4. Approve the trustline transaction.

StellarTerm

  1. Sign in with your preferred Stellar wallet.
  2. Open balances or accept asset controls.
  3. Add OGC using the issuer account above.
  4. Confirm the trustline before submitting your address.

Stellar Lab

  1. Use the Build Transaction tools on public network.
  2. Create a Change Trust operation for OGC and the issuer.
  3. Set a limit that matches your intended participation.
  4. Sign with your receiving wallet and submit.

After You Add the Trustline

  1. Submit your public Stellar address through the airdrop pilot form.
  2. Open Build validates account existence and OGC trustline status locally.
  3. Approved batches are signed manually from the published distribution wallet.
  4. Distribution results are added to the public transparency log.
